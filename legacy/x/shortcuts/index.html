Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Edit Page E

    Jumps cursor to the editor. Your text saves automatically and updates in realtime. You can also just tap the page to edit.

  • Search Pages S

    Opens the search bar at the top for quick page navigation.

  • Copy Content C

    Copies all visible text content on the current page to your clipboard.

  • Copy Link L

    Copies the current page's URL to your clipboard for instant sharing.

  • Go Home H

    Navigates directly back to the home page. It may also be a duck.

  • QR Code Q

    Shows or hides the QR code for the page.

  • Escape Esc

    Leaves the editor, search bar, or QR code modal.
